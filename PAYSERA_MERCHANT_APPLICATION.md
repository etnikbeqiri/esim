# Paysera Merchant Application Answers

## Project/Service Name

**MerrSim** - Digital eSIM Marketplace Platform

## Detailed Description of the Project

MerrSim is a B2B/B2C e-commerce platform that specializes in selling digital eSIM (embedded SIM) data packages for international travelers. Our service enables customers to purchase and activate mobile data plans electronically without the need for physical SIM cards.

### What We Sell

We offer digital eSIM data packages that include:

- **Regional and Global eSIM Plans**: Mobile data packages covering single countries, multiple regions (Europe, Asia, Americas), and global coverage
- **Data-Only Packages**: Plans ranging from 1GB to 20GB of mobile data with validity periods from 7 to 30 days
- **Instant Activation**: Customers receive their eSIM QR code immediately after successful payment, allowing instant activation on their eSIM-compatible devices

### Products Available on Our Website

| Product Type | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Country eSIMs** | Single-country data packages for travelers visiting specific destinations |
| **Regional eSIMs** | Multi-country coverage (e.g., Europe, Asia) for travelers visiting multiple countries |
| **Global eSIMs** | Worldwide coverage for frequent travelers |
| **Data Packages** | Various data allowances (1GB, 3GB, 5GB, 10GB, 20GB) with different validity periods |

### How Our Service Works

1. **Browse**: Customer selects their destination country/region and desired data package
2. **Purchase**: Customer completes secure payment via Paysera
3. **Delivery**: Upon successful payment, customer receives an eSIM QR code via email and dashboard
4. **Activation**: Customer scans the QR code on their eSIM-compatible device to activate mobile data

### Business Model

- **B2C**: Direct sales to end consumers (travelers) via card payment
- **B2B**: Wholesale sales to travel agencies and resellers who use prepaid account balances

All products are **digital goods** delivered instantly via email and online dashboard. No physical shipping is involved.

## Delivery Terms

### Digital Delivery Policy

Since MerrSim sells **exclusively digital products** (eSIM profiles and data packages), there is no physical shipping involved. Delivery is instant and automated:

| Delivery Method | Timeframe |
|-----------------|-----------|
| **Email** | Instant (within 1-2 minutes after successful payment) |
| **Customer Dashboard** | Instant (immediately available after payment) |
| **QR Code** | Provided immediately as both downloadable image and inline display |

### Delivery Process

1. Upon successful payment confirmation from Paysera, our system automatically generates a unique eSIM QR code
2. The QR code is sent to the customer's email address provided during checkout
3. The same QR code is available in the customer's account dashboard for future access
4. Customer scans the QR code using their eSIM-compatible smartphone to activate the mobile data plan

**No physical delivery or shipping is required.**

## Refund Policy

### MerrSim Refund Policy

#### Digital Nature of Products

Please note that eSIM products are **digital goods** that are delivered instantly upon purchase. Due to the nature of these products, our refund policy is more restrictive than for physical goods.

#### Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be issued in the following cases:

| Scenario | Refund Available |
|----------|------------------|
| **Technical Failure** | Yes - If our system fails to deliver the eSIM QR code |
| **Incorrect Product Delivered** | Yes - If a different package than ordered is delivered |
| **Defective eSIM** | Yes - If the eSIM cannot be activated due to a technical error on our end |
| **Changed Mind** | No - Once the QR code has been delivered/viewed |
| **Incompatible Device** | No - Customers must verify eSIM compatibility before purchase |
| **Used eSIM** | No - Once an eSIM has been activated/used, no refund is possible |

#### Refund Request Process

1. Customer must submit a refund request within **24 hours** of purchase
2. Requests must be sent to our support team with order details
3. We will verify the issue (delivery failure, technical error, etc.)
4. Approved refunds are processed within **5-7 business days** back to the original payment method

#### Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be considered in cases where:
- Multiple eSIMs were purchased and only some were used
- Pro-rata refund for unused portion (at company's discretion)

#### Non-Refundable Situations

No refunds will be issued for:
- Customer's device is not eSIM-compatible
- Customer's device is network-locked to a different carrier
- Customer purchased an eSIM for the wrong country/region
- Customer simply changed their mind after receiving the QR code
- eSIM has already been activated or used
